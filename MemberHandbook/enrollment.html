Enrollment

I have been accepted into the NCC—now what?

. Policy

In order to attend any NCC session or participate in any NCC event, a student must be ENROLLED in the program. Being “enrolled” is strictly defined by the following:

  • All required electronic forms are completed and received by the NCC.
  • $95 Registration Fee is received by the NCC.
  • Tuition Fee has been received by the NCC, or automatic monthly payments have been set up.

Important: If any of the above requirements are not met, a student is not considered “enrolled,” and therefore will not be permitted to participate in any NCC rehearsal, performance or event.

. Paying Tuition

Pay in Full and Save 5%

Opt to pay your tuition fees in full at the beginning of your contract, and save 5% off full-year enrollments.

Pay Monthly

For your convenience, the NCC offers monthly tuition payments with an easy online sign-up process. For a one-time set-up fee of $45, our secure system will automatically deduct a monthly payment from your debit or credit card, allowing you to spread your payments over twelve (12) months, increasing affordability and flexibility to families that prefer it. Here are some important things to remember when signing up for monthly tuition payments ( read carefully)â€”

  • Once the enrollment contract is signed, you are legally committed to paying the full year’s tuition, regardless of whether your child leaves the program or not.
  • Once begun, monthly payments will be automatically deducted from your debit or credit card and cannot be stopped.
  • Monthly payments are made over twelve (12) months, even though the NCC performance season is over ten (10) months; this is done to make the monthly amount less, and create additional affordability for you. This may mean that you see charges for July and August (when the NCC is not in session), depending on when you began your twelve (12) month contract.
  • Should monthly payments go into arrears at any point, the account must be brought current immediately in order for your child to attend rehearsal or any NCC event.

Important: Monthly payments spread over twelve (12) months must begin by the deadline specified for each season in order to align correctly with the end of instruction. Payments continuing on past the end of the final season will not be permitted.

. Tuition Adjustments

In the event your child is promoted to a new NCC level in the middle of your contract, assuming the new ensemble has a different tuition amount, you will receive an e-mail with a link to make an adjustment, prorated to reflect the difference in cost for the applicable seasons of participation. Here is how it works—

  • The total annual amount for your current contract is divided into three (3) - Fall, Winter and Spring.
  • The total annual amount for the new ensemble’s tuition is divided into three (3) - Fall, Winter and Spring.
  • A new total annual amount is created by adding the applicable amounts for each season of participation in the corresponding level (i.e. Fall + Winter of current contract, plus Spring of the new ensemble’s tuition).
  • Finally, the current contract’s total annual amount is subtracted from the new total annual amount. The difference is what you will pay for the adjustment.
