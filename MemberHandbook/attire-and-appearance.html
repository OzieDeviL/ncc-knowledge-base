Attire & Appearance

Looking the part

. Vision

At the National Children’s Chorus, professionalism is exemplified on every level. This most certainly includes the powerful visual image our singers give to the general public at concerts and various performances. For this reason, in collaboration with Dennis Uniform Company, we have developed a spectacular look for our students!

. NCC Formal Dress

  • Will be used for all major NCC performances, events and ceremonies.
  • Cost: Approximately $85 for boys and $95 for girls (which includes a 15% bulk order discount already secured for you by the NCC).
  • How to obtain: Use the measurements taken when you were fitted during your NCC Audition. Follow the directions listed to place the order online. If you need to be fitted again, please contact our Executive Administrator who will assist you with making appropriate arrangements; payment is made directly to Dennis.

What Each Student Needs

  1. One complete NCC Uniform Set (either for boy or girl), as ordered through Dennis Uniform Company which includes a white shirt, a black knit vest with NCC logo, black pants and belt (for boys), and black skirt and stockings (for girls).
  2. Boys—Black socks.
  3. Boys—Black shoes must be black dress shoes with black soles, black laces and black lace rings (no gold, silver or shiny anything).
  4. Girls—Black shoes must be closed toe dress shoes with black soles (no gold, silver or shiny anything).

Policy

With the NCC Uniform, we are striving to obtain a single and consistent look for all our students. Therefore, substitutions are not allowed for any of the items listed, and the exact garment piece as ordered through Dennis Uniform Company is required for all NCC performances and formal events. The only item (other than black socks for boys) that is not included in the package is shoes, which must meet the criteria as described.

Please Note: For various reasons, some students elect to wear a plain white T-shirt underneath the Dennis long-sleeved white dress shirt. This is fine, as long as the T-shirt is a V-neck, and may not be visible from the outside once the full uniform is on.

. NCC Casual Dress

  • Will be used for all rehearsals (LA only), field trips (LA & NY) and other off-campus events (LA & NY).
  • Cost: NCC T-shirts are $35 each.
  • How to obtain: T-shirts are available for purchase online at My NCC.

What Each Student Needs

  1. NCC T-shirt.
  2. Blue Jeans.
  3. White sneakers.
  4. White long-sleeve thermal T-shirt (to be worn under the NCC T-shirt on cold days).

. NCC Festival Dress

  • Will be used for all major non-NCC performances, events and ceremonies.

What Each Student Needs

  1. Festival dress is the same as NCC Formal Dress, but without the black knit vest.

. Grooming for Performance

Girls

  • White long sleeve shirt, NCC vest, skirt and tights, all provided by Dennis Uniform Company.
  • Collar is to be buttoned down to the shirt at its points, but the top button of the shirt is to be left open.
  • Black, closed-toe shoes.
  • Hair must be up in a bun— No ponytails; small hair accessories are fine, but must be black, or the color of the hair.
  • No scented hairspray, scented products, perfume or cologne; no sparkly/glitter hairspray; unscented hair product is allowed, and encouraged to be used wherever necessary.
  • No nail polish, except clear or french tips.
  • No jewelry, rings or sparkly anything; discreet stud earrings are allowed only if cannot be removed (no earrings preferred).
  • No bracelets; no watches.
  • Make-up is a must on stage, but can be minimal—simply add some color to lips and cheeks to prevent looking washed-out under stage lighting.

Boys

  • White long sleeve shirt, NCC vest, pants and black belt, all provided by Dennis Uniform Company.
  • Collar is to be buttoned down to the shirt at its points, but the top button is to be left open.
  • Black dress shoes, with black socks.
  • No bracelets; no watches.
  • Hair must be combed with unscented gel or product to hold in place; please look as neat and clean as possible.
  • No scented hairspray, scented products, perfume or cologne.
  • No jewelry, rings or sparkly anything.
