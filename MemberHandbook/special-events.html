Special Events

. Annual NCC Awards Ceremony

Recognizing Our Students’ Exceptional Achievement

Each year in June, the NCC holds its annual Awards Ceremonies, both in Los Angeles and New York. These special events are designed to honor and recognize the stellar accomplishments of our gifted students which have been realized throughout the year. In celebration of the season, awards are given out for participation and other various distinctions. As this event is of paramount importance to the students, providing a special occasion to acknowledge their excellence, we ask that each NCC family makes sure to reserve this date several months in advance to avoid any potential scheduling conflicts.

Who is Invited?

Due to space and capacity restrictions, the Awards Reception guest list is limited to the following—

  • All students enrolled during the Fall, Winter and Spring Seasons of the current performance year.
  • 2 adult guests per student (additional guests are placed on a wait list and confirmed the week of the event based on availability).
  • Donors of the Conductor’s Circle “Patron Level” or higher ($2500 and above) Dress Code for the Awards Reception.

Dress Code for the Awards Reception

  • All students at the Awards Reception are to be in NCC Formal Dress, including grooming requirements. Annual NCC Awards Ceremony.

Awards

The NCC recognizes its students with the following distinctions—

Honor Ribbons

  • White Ribbon: Participation in the Prelude Level.
  • Gray Ribbon: Participation in the Minuet Level.
  • Red Ribbon: Participation in the Sonata Levl.
  • Silver Ribbon: Participation in the Debut Ensemble.
  • Gold Ribbon: Participation in the Premier Ensemble.
  • Black Ribbon: Participation in the Scholars.
  • Yellow Ribbon: Winner of a solo role in any ensemble.
  • Royal Blue Ribbon: Participation in the Conducting Fellows.
  • Purple Ribbon: NCC Regional Audition Representative.
  • Hot Pink Ribbon: Fundraiser Participant.
  • Aqua Blue Ribbon: Participation in Professional Studio Work.
  • Pale Pink Ribbon: Participation in the Advanced Private Vocal Studio.
  • Green Ribbon: Participation in the Intensive Composition Workshop.

Apple Awards

As awarded throughout the year for monthly achievement.

Attendance Award

Zero (0) absences, tardy arrivals or early dismissals for the entire year.

Director’s Choice Participation Award.

For finest level of participation and overall classroom contribution throughout the year.

  • One (1) student from each ensemble.

Director’s Choice Development Award.

For finest overall improvement over the course of the year.

  • One (1) student from each ensemble.

Director’s Choice Citizenship Award.

For finest showing in all areas of rehearsal and performance within the year, exceeding expectations and consistently going above and beyond what is required.

  • One (1) student from each ensemble.

Please note: Additional Ribbon categories may be added throughout the year as necessary.
