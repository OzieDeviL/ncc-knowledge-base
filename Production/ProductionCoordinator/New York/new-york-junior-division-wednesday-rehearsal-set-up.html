New York Junior Division Wednesday Rehearsal Set-up

Creating the space for greatness

. Vision

A rehearsal space which is set up perfectly allows the conductor and the students to focus completely on the musical and educational process. The set-up of any space is a key factor in the success of a rehearsal.

. Procedures

Check-in Desk

Check-in Desk

  • Position two (2) large table and three (3) chairs in Room 101 to the left of the door as you enter the room.
  • Table cloth — place on table so logo is centered and straight, hanging in front.
  • Check-in/Check-out sheets (CICOS) for ensembles — attach to clipboards.
  • Pens — one placed on each clipboard.
  • NCC T-shirts for students — fold and place neatly in a stack on the Check-in Desk.
  • Square reader/cashbox — place under Check-in Desk for easy accessibility.
  • NCC Hoodies — fold a few hoodies and place neatly in a stack on the Check-in Desk.
  • DVDs — place neatly in a stack on Check-in Desk (have DVD paid list available to reference; if not on paid list, can also purchase for $25).
  • Red Chaperone Badges — place neatly on Check-in desk and give to designated chaperones as they arrive.
  • Red Staff Badges - visibly wear your personalized name badge or an NCC Authorized Personnel badge for identification.
  • Emergency Binder — place under the Check-in Desk for easy accessibility.
  • Season Schedules — place neatly in a stack on Check-in Desk, separated by ensemble.
  • Bookmarks — place neatly in a stack next to Season Schedules.
  • Concert postcards (once received) — place neatly in a stack in the center of Check-in Desk.
  • No electronics — remind students to put any cell phones and electronics away for rehearsal, otherwise they will be taken.

Room 101 (Dedicated check-in/check-out room)

  • Make sure all lights are turned on.
  • Make sure temperature is moderate (not too hot or cold; adjust as needed with SPA maintenance staff).
  • Make sure Check-in Desk is set up properly.
  • T-Shirt Check — ask any student not wearing an NCC t-shirt where the t-shirt is and why s/he does not have it. Then notate this by the student’s name on the CICOS.
  • Text Chelsea Morel immediately at 760.831.4505 if any instructor is late (even by 1 minute) and/or does not arrive in dress code (all black attire).
  • Text Chelsea Morel immediately if any issues arise during rehearsal.

Music Room (LL1) - Minuet Level

Chair set-up, Music Room

Pianist's set-up, Music Room

Materials set-up, Music Room

  • Make sure all lights are turned on.
  • Set up portable stand and upright piano.
  • Set up keyboard bench.
  • Set up dry erase board and tripod in front of countertop.
  • Place NCC sign on countertop facing the door, so people see it when they enter.
  • Set up 31 chairs in 4 rows.
  • Place Kodály supplies (dry erase pens, white boards and cloths) to the side at the front of the room, easily accessible to students.

LL2 Prelude Level

Table and Chair set-up, LL2

Materials set-up, LL2

  • Make sure all lights are turned on.
  • Clear all chairs and table; clean floor with Swiffer Wet Jet.
  • Set up portable stand and keyboard.
  • Set up keyboard bench.
  • Place NCC sign on piano facing the door, so people see it when they enter.
  • Place pencil box on piano, easily accessible to students.
  • Place accompanist binder on piano music stand.
  • Place portable dry erase board and stand in front of room.
  • Place dry erase markers by dry erase board.

Chaperones and Break Supervision

  • Position a chaperone by Room 101 to monitor the restrooms from 4pm-4:30pm.
  • Position another chaperone downstairs to monitor LL2 and the Music Room. Place additional chaperones as needed.
  • Chaperones and NCC staff should monitor the students at all times during break to ensure they are behaving professionally. No physical contact should occur between students at break. Note any physical contact in weekly report.
  • After 4:30 PM, once rehearsal has started, confiscate any electronics. Any confiscated electronic devices must be returned to the parent, not the student. Please remind the parent that elecronics are not to be used at the NCC.

Rehearsal Wrap-up

  • Clean up any trash from rehearsal rooms.
  • Check all rehearsal rooms for left belongings.
  • Gather all materials from Room 101, Music Room and LL2 and return to the office.
  • Scan CICOS and scan to Aaron at ajakubiec@ncchorus.com.

. Results

A successfully set up and executed rehearsal will result in a smooth, enjoyable experience for conductors, students, parents and staff.
