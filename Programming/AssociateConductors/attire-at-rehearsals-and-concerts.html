Attire at Rehearsal and Concerts

How to dress at the NCC

. Vision

NCC Conductors and Instructors are ambassadors for the culture and artistic standards of the National Children's Chorus. Attire at rehearsals and events should be reflective of the excellence of the organization and its mission.

. Procedures

Rehearsal Attire

  • Please wear all black at NCC rehearsals: Black pants, black shirt, black shoes, black socks.
  • Heels no higher than one (1) inch.
  • You will also receive an NCC silver pin (magnetic). Please place this on your shirt at each rehearsal to signify that you are a conductor/instructor.
  • No bare shoulders.
  • No bare legs.
  • Hair neatly groomed.
